connecting interactors worldwide

Initiative Interchange is a platform that helps members of the Rotary youth organization Interact from all around the world to connect with one another. Our goals are developing strong friendships as well as organizing get-togethers to foster international cooperation and collaboration.

one common goal

We want our cooperation to make a lasting impact. To this end, every single donation collected by Interact Clubs partaking in our network is used for our shared goal: primary school education for everyone.

Together, we aim to raise a total of €1,000,000 towards the construction of schools – a sum which enables us to provide 200,000 children with access to the basic human right of education.
